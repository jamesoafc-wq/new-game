# Leisure Club Tycoon 3D - Expanded Design

## Fantasy

Build a tired empty leisure unit into a premium health, gym, spa and wellness club. The vibe is not a pure gym; it should feel like David Lloyd / Nuffield / private members club / resort spa mixed with a tycoon builder.

## Core loop

1. Build facilities.
2. Members visit and spend money.
3. Club rating rises or falls based on variety, cleanliness, crowding, condition and luxury feel.
4. Better rating increases membership and reputation.
5. Reputation unlocks premium facilities.
6. Use cash to expand the plot and upgrade the club.

## Member archetypes

- New Starter: cheap, easy to satisfy, mainly cardio/classes.
- Gym Regular: uses weights/cardio, dislikes crowding.
- Swimmer: wants pool/changing rooms, pays steady fees.
- Spa Guest: high spend, expects sauna/spa/cleanliness.
- Class Fan: wants yoga/spin/pilates timetable.
- Family Member: values pool, cafe, creche and cleanliness.
- Corporate Member: pays well, expects premium lounge and fast check-in.

## Deeper systems to add

### Room zoning
Players should eventually draw rooms using walls, floors, doors and windows. Facilities should work best inside correct zones.

### Staff
- Receptionist: faster check-in, higher first impression.
- Cleaner: slows cleanliness decay.
- Instructor: improves studio/cardio/weights value.
- Lifeguard: required for bigger pools.
- Therapist: required for spa income.
- Manager: improves efficiency and reduces complaints.

### Progression
- Level 1: Independent local gym.
- Level 2: Health club.
- Level 3: Leisure club with pool.
- Level 4: Premium spa club.
- Level 5: Resort-style wellness campus.

### Events
- January fitness rush.
- Summer pool surge.
- Equipment breakdown.
- Bad review going viral.
- Local competitor opens.
- Corporate membership offer.
- Health inspection.
- Celebrity/influencer visit.

## Visual direction

- Clean glass walls.
- Warm wood.
- Soft teal lighting.
- Premium stone floors.
- Plants and water features.
- Spa/pool areas glowing slightly at night.
- Members visible as simple stylised characters at first.

## Long-term dream version

A full 3D leisure club builder where you can design multi-floor buildings, roof terraces, pools, saunas, treatment rooms, cafes, indoor courts and outdoor wellness gardens. Members behave differently, reviews appear dynamically and your club brand grows from a small local gym to a national luxury chain.
